

## Equiv. of sol.

Assume $|x|,\|x\|$ are positively homogeneous.
$$
\begin{cases}
\max |x|\\
st.\|x\|=1
\end{cases}
$$
dual problem:
$$
\begin{cases}
\min \|x\|\\
st.|x|=1
\end{cases}
$$

Define $x^*=x/|x|, x^+=x/\|x\|$.

$x$ is the sol. of (1), then $\|x^*\||x|=1$. We have $\|x^*\|=\frac{1}{|x|}\leq \frac{1}{|z^+|}=\|z\|$. Hence $x^*$ is the sol. of (2). vice verse.
